Welcome to ELROND 2038 world

Elrond 2038 is an ecological project that aims to support associations working for sustainable development.

What is Elrond 2038 ?

Elrond 2038 is a collection of eco-friendly properties SFTs unique digital collectibles living on the Elrond blockchain. By obtaining an Elrond 2038 NFT collectible, you are helping charities, but you can also create your own eco-friendly city and make it grow.

Save the world

By obtaining an Elrond 2038 NFT collectible, you are helping to save our planet as part of the proceeds will go towards a joint project with a charitable organization.

Today, 4 billion people, more than half of humanity, do not have access to renewable energy. They therefore depend on polluting energy sources, such as coal or wood. Our project is to provide access to renewable energy in developing countries through the construction of biogas tanks, or solar cookers for example.

Collect properties

The properties are both futuristic and eco-responsible buildings. They are an image of the world we hope to see in a few years.

Get one or more properties during our weekly drops. To do so, you just have to join our discord and follow our instructions. If all the properties are sold out, you can always buy them directly on the secondary market.

Step into a new dimension

By becoming a property owner, you gain access to our game. Now you can build your own city and bring projects to life.

Contribute to projects in real life

When you own a property, you will be free to set it up wherever you want in our game. Depending on where you place your property, you finance a project in reality. This way, you will receive regular news about the project but you will also be able to follow its progress in real time directly from our game.

Upgrade your properties

All properties are available in different versions and can be improved. By improving a property, you allow it to produce more energy. Please note that the enhanced versions are very limited. Only the fastest will be able to access them.

What is the total supply ?

+

The Genesis collection is composed of 12 different properties. Each property is available in 256 copies in its basic version. Improved versions of these properties will be much rarer and available in smaller quantities.
